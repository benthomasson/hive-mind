## Adding a Channel

1. Add the channel to the appropriate table above
2. Associate with a topic if applicable
3. Use `slacker` to monitor:
   ```bash
   uvx --from "git+https://github.com/shanemcd/slacker" slacker search "in:#channel-name today"
   ```
